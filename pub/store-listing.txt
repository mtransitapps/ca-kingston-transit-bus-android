Full description:
This application adds Kingston Transit buses information to MonTransit.

Kingston Transit serves Kingston & Amherstview in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Kingston Transit.
https://www.cityofkingston.ca/residents/transit/about

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-kingston-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with the Corporation of the City of Kingston and Kingston Transit.
